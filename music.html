MUSIC

Here's the Music we've put out

We've been a band for 6 years, and all we have to show for it are these 14 lousy songs.


hi. (2015)

After releasing Peace of Mind we toured for a bit and then took an extended break to get our bearings. Working on hi. gave us the chance to regroup and have a delightful time recording new songs we loved with no pressure.


Peace of Mind (2014)

Our sophomore effort. This EP is like when you had to write essays in high school supporting an argument opposite the one you'd naturally take. You know you worked really hard on it, and if you remember correctly it came out great, but you can't fucking stand to go back and check it out.


The Spring Tour EP (2013)

Our first EP, hastily written, recorded, and released moments before we left for our first tour in March of 2013
